West Yorkshire Python User Group Meeting - Thurs. 15th Jan

This week we have a talk from Tony Simpson

Micropython

In the first of three meetups focused on Python for micro controller programming Tony will introduce us to the Micropython board, how to get started programming the board, key differences between Micropython and cPython and throw out some ideas for projects based on Micropython.

Micropython is a great entry point to low level programming for Python programmers. Micropython has similar hardware to Arduino but is more accessable due to being focused on Python.

Tony is an experienced Python hacker who loves new and interesting problems

Who's it for?

This talk is aimed at those new to Micropython.

About the Group

The West Yorkshire Python User Group (WYPy) have been meeting monthly since 2007. Our meetings are free, and usually include at least one talk, as well as a trip to the pub.

Our website is at http://wypy.org.uk . We discuss our meetings on the Python Yorkshire and Humberside Google Group http://groups.google.com/group/python-yorks-humber/ and you can also follow us on Twitter at @WYPython.
